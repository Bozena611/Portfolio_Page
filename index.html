aboutMe

After working as an English and Spanish teacher and translator for six years, I decided to follow my passion for Sustainable development and obtained an MA in Environment and Sustainable Development from Erasmus University Rotterdam in 2014.

Since then I have expanded my knowledge in the area of Circular Economy and Zero Waste and have been collaborating on various projects with InovationPark - a startup in development, where I have been developing creative and sustainable ideas for solving waste management problems, which are in line with Circular Economy.

Having creative ideas for applications and games related to Sustainability, naturally lead me to exploring the world of coding. In the end I decided to dive into it fully and enrolled in the JavaScript Full-Stack Bootcamp at Barcelona Code School, where I learned the MERN Stack.

Throughout the bootcamp I discovered the joy of making your code work and now feel excited about new professional opportunities in web/mobile app development.

I am looking for a junior position in a company which will support my coding development. I am eager to improve my coding skills and gain new knowledge in both backend and frontend field.
